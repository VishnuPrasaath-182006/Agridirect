Buy & Sell Crops with Government

Connecting farmers to official procurement channels with ease and transparency.

Government Procurement Schemes

AgriGrow helps farmers register and participate in government procurement schemes like MSP (Minimum Support Price) for crops like wheat, rice, and pulses. Our platform verifies your PM-KISAN ID and bank details securely to ensure quick payments.

Farmer Registration & Validation

Farmers can easily register through our portal by submitting required documents. Our system validates your eligibility with government records, ensuring you're eligible for direct crop sales and benefits.

Crop Listing & Scheduling

List your crops with quantity, expected price, and availability. Our dashboard notifies government procurement agencies and schedules logistics for pickup or delivery based on location.

Guaranteed Payments

Once your crops are received and verified, payments are directly deposited to your registered bank account under government timelines. AgriGrow tracks your sale and ensures full transparency.
